My Experience Designing a Personal Site

Dec 18, 2015

What was it like designing this site?

I have designed this site from scratch. It initially just started as a simple site with a blogging section. However, over time I have added more pieces such as the about me page, projects pages and a contact page. It was great building those things over time, mostly because I coded it from scratch. It served a learning experience. I like the fact that I customized each aspect myself from the wireframe up, allowing me to creatively express myself and make something that reflects me. I hope as a visitor of this site, people will recognize that it is unique, simple and minimal. This is the vision I had for my site to begin with. I did not use any frameworks such as Jekyll or Bootstrap to customize my site because I am quite happy with the way it looks and I personally think that using one of those frameworks does not really make my site unique. Perhaps in the future I will change the theme and go with a more "professional" looking site, but for now I am happy with the way it looks, responds and serves its purpose.
